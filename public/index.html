Available Fields:

  • Execution, LastName, FirstName, TDCJNumber
  • Age, Race, CountyOfConviction, AgeWhenReceived
  • EducationLevel, NativeCounty, PreviousCrime, Codefendants
  • NumberVictim, WhiteVictim, HispanicVictim, BlackVictim
  • VictimOtherRaces, FemaleVictim, MaleVictim, LastStatement

Search Examples:

  • FirstName = Paul - First name is Paul.
  • Race = Black - Records where race is Black.
  • Age > 30 OR Age < 20 - Age above 30 or below 20.
  • Race = Black AND NOT CountyOfConviction = Harris - Exclude Harris County.
  • (Race = White AND Age >= 40) OR (Race = Hispanic AND NumberVictim > 2) - Combine multiple criteria.

Comparison Operators:

  • =, !=, <, >, =<, =>

Tips:

  • Combine conditions using AND, OR, and NOT.
  • Group conditions with parentheses for clarity.
  • Search is case-insensitive.
